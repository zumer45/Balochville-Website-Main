Hey there, I'm Balochville

Balochville is a channel founded by Adnan Umer, a Baloch immigrant who migrated from Pakistan to the United States and currently living in a small rural town in Central Arkansas. The channel showcases the countryside life of American people living in villages, small towns, and rural areas away from the hustle and bustle of the cities. You'll see how Adnan, as a Pakistani Baloch immigrant, manages to live in that environment on a day-to-day basis. You'll also get to join me on trips to remote and rural areas of Arkansas and other US states, and eventually other countries in the future. Plus, I'll be sharing some finger-licking good desi food recipes too, because cooking is my passion and I love to cook!
